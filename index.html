Recent Events

Our Bit by Bit team leaders helped State Farm organize a balloon launch event for underprivileged students, fostering their interest in technology. Through hands-on activities and interactive demonstrations, our leaders introduced students to weather tracking technologies and coding principles. This collaborative effort inspired a passion for STEM subjects and opened doors to future educational and career opportunities.

Year in Recap:
October: 1st meeting with all schools
November: Introduction to block-coding for middle school; SF Quantum Computing presentation for high school
December: Create your own story activity in Scratch for middle school; No meeting for high school
January: Introduction to python for middle school; Introduced Retro-Cade competition for high school
February: Introduced Retro-Cade competition for middle school; VEX Robots activity for high school
March: Worked on competition projects for middle school; Arduino workshop for high school
April: Retro-Cade Conference on April 8th

A very successful and exciting first year in Bit-by-Bit!
